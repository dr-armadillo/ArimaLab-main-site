ABOUT US

Enzymes are proteins that catalyze the chemical reactions necessary for all living organisms to survive. Their function is essential for the maintenance of life in all organisms, and people have unconsciously utilized enzymes through the process of fermentation since ancient times. Taking a broad view of our surroundings, various enzymes and microorganisms contribute to industries and daily life. In our laboratory, we are dedicated to discovering useful enzymes through microbial fermentation and exploring their applications in various fields such as the effective utilization of untapped resources and manufacturing.
